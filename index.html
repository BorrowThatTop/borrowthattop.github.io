@Borrow That Top

A simple Bot that interacts with Twitter users based on keywords used on status updates. The Bot uses Twitter Firehose to collect status updates via the Twitter Stream. He is friendly so if you follow him he will follow you back.

About @Borrow That Top

@Borrow That Top was created to demo the use of the Twitter firehose streaming API. It was developed by PHP Programmer Angel S. Moreno from PHPMine. The bot was inspired by Kelly, a character portrayed by American comedian, actor, and director Liam Kyle Sullivan.
